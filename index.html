About Phoenix University Agwada

Located in Agwada, Nasarawa State, Phoenix University offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs, fostering innovation and practical skills. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and industry, preparing students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Academic Programs

We offer programs across various faculties, including Allied Health, Social and Management Sciences, Computing and Technology, and Natural, Applied Sciences, and Agriculture. Explore our undergraduate and postgraduate degrees tailored to meet industry demands.
